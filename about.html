COMPANY HISTORY

Dream and Dream Big...

One should never forget their roots because it always has that positive connection lying deep within one. They say ''What you sow is what you reap’’ and that is exactly what has happened with me too. Initially what started as a simple yet small journey, turned out to be an emotional and sentimental one. Everything seems like yesterday when I took that long stroll and stumbled upon this place in my neighborhood of Wayanad, Kerala. I believe I was destined to own this land. Buying this piece of land directly or indirectly was a blessing in disguise for me as well as my friend who sold it to me. No major plans were sketched out to transform this into anything, definitely not to a villa. The one thing that astounded and surprised me was the way in which this place drastically grew into. I wondered how and this caretaker that I appointed to take care of the land, vested all her efforts to ensure that she waters and takes care of the place with her heart and soul.

SOMETHING ABOUT US

Taking this inspiration from her I decided to make things better by transforming this place into a dream project. Hurdles were always a constant thing but I love challenges. My vision was to recreate this place into a wonderful and amazing masterpiece. Frankly speaking, it consumed a lot of time and now, finally, it is ready to welcome people with both open arms. Abigail Villa is what it is called! In short, it is called - Ab's Villa. The time that I invested in bringing this dream project of mine into reality was, is, and forever will be worth every single penny. It’s just that I have an emotional connection with this villa. The time I spent with my mother, my family members, and naming it after my daughter and everything has a close bond. It has that home touch in every nook and corner which will give you a soothing comfort throughout your stay. Now to the best part of the villa, it has amazing features and specifications that make it one of a kind villa. It is built within a vast area that is in the midst of nature's comfort zone. It has a serene touch of cool breeze and calmness which is far from all the buzz of city life. Silver Oak, Paddy Fields, Banana Trees, Areca nuts, coconut trees, and a wide range of other trees surround the villa. What matters to me as an individual who owns the property is that I want the people coming in to share their experiences and also leave the place with a sense of inner peace and satisfaction.

Villa composes of a splendid living room that displays a magnificent touch of technology. The living room also has a dining area and a modular kitchen setup that helps in cooking and preparing your dishes. This modular kitchen will give you all the required equipment to test your skills at preparing a master recipe at any time of the day. The villa also has two separate bedrooms with king-size beds where the cots are made up of teak wood. Speaking of teakwood, the furniture in the entire villa is carved out of teakwood. We want to safeguard nature and ensure that we in some manner contribute to nature. The Bedrooms boast attached restrooms and an open bathroom that gives you a unique feel of devolving in nature.The villa also has a bar counter for guests to sit, relax and gulp down a few drinks. It has a dedicated library section for bookworms and a balcony that allows you to set your eyes on the beautiful sunset and sunrise. We have poured out our hearts and sweat to ensure that every minute detail is attended to. We believe that it should act out as a place to retreat rather than just to cater to your entertainment needs. Listening to the creaking sounds of nightlight while sitting and recalling the day’s adventures besides the campfire is another amazing experience
